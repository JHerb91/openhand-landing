How it works

Three steps, then you stop searching.

There's no feed to scroll. You show up once, and OpenHand does the looking.

01

Complete your profile

Your personality, and your Giving Profile — what you bring to a relationship, not just what you're looking for.

02

Live your life

OpenHand searches quietly in the background. No daily swiping required to stay in the pool.

03

Get introduced, with reasons

When there's a real fit, you're introduced — and told exactly why. Talk first; request a photo only when you're both ready.

For organizers

Bring a different kind of singles night.

Event Mode is a free pilot for one NWA organizer, church, or venue. Attendees join with a private code and are matched only with others at your event.

"We provide the private matching layer for your singles event. Attendees enter your event code, match only with other attendees, receive an explanation of why they fit, and can choose whether to start a conversation."

Where we're starting

Northwest Arkansas first — on purpose.

OpenHand isn't launching statewide. A concentrated local pool means real people, nearby, worth actually meeting — not installs scattered across a state with no one to match.

  • Priority 1Bentonville · Rogers · Springdale · FayettevilleYoung professionals, university-connected adults, active churches, board-game and arts venues.
  • Priority 2Little Rock · North Little RockSingles ministries and young-professional networks — a second, separate local pool.
